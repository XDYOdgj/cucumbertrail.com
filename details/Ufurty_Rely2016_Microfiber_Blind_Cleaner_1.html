  • Designed with an ergonomic handle for comfortable grip and smooth maneuverability

  • Dual sleeves offer versatility for cleaning different blind styles efficiently

Why You Need It

Unlike traditional cleaning tools that can scratch or leave residue behind, the Ufurty Rely2016 uses soft microfiber to trap dirt and dust while preserving the integrity of your blinds. Whether you're cleaning window blinds, air conditioner panels, or other difficult-to-reach areas in your home or office, this cleaner is a valuable addition to your cleaning arsenal. It helps you achieve a clean and fresh living space with minimal effort and maximum results.

Whether you're looking for a blind cleaner, a venetian blind brush, or a window duster, the Ufurty Rely2016 is built to deliver.
