Caboo Tree Free Napkins

Looking for a sustainable and eco-friendly alternative to traditional paper napkins? Caboo offers tree-free napkins that are both kind to the planet and perfect for everyday use. Made from rapidly renewable viscose, these napkins provide a greener solution without compromising on quality or performance.

These biodegradable napkins come in a convenient 1-ply format that's surprisingly strong and absorbent. Whether you're dealing with spills at home, messy meals on the go, or need a reliable option for camping or travel, they're always up to the task. Free from BPA, lint, and fragrance, they're safe for your family, pets, and even sensitive skin.

Caboo's innovative approach ensures a durable, lightweight, and versatile product that fits into your lifestyle. With a bulk pack of 4 (250 sheets each), you get 1000 napkins ideal for everyday messes, kitchen use, or outdoor adventures. They're also certified with BRC, FSC, FDA, and ISO standards, making them a trusted choice for eco-conscious consumers who value sustainability and quality.

Another great feature of these napkins is their Panda Friendly status. Since the viscose used isn't a food source for pandas, Caboo helps protect these endangered animals while offering you a reliable, environmentally responsible solution. Choose Caboo Tree Free Napkins for a greener, more mindful way to manage messes and keep your space clean.
